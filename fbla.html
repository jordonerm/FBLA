CHERRY CREEK HIGH SCHOOL: FUTURE BUSINESS LEADERS OF AMERICA

What is FBLA?

FBLA (Future Business Leaders of America) is an educational club at Creek that combines business and education together in an enjoyable manner. With the experience from business classes at Creek, students can compete in various events and competitions up to a national level. For more information view the FBLA website:

www.FBLA-PBL.org

How to join:

In order to be apart of FBLA at Cherry Creek one must have completed and passed a business course offered by Creek. Visit our stand at the Club Fair to learn more about registering to be a member of FBLA Cherry Creek.

These are the courses offered:

  • Accounting 1
  • Accounting 2
  • Business Law 1
  • Business Law 2
  • Computer Applications
  • Introduction to Business
  • Marketing 1
  • Marketing 2
  • Personal Finance
